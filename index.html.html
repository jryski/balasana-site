The Journey to Balance

Balasana started as a dream to create wellness apparel that embodied balance and mindfulness. The name itself—Sanskrit for "child's pose"—represented our commitment to finding equilibrium in an increasingly chaotic world.

But as we researched materials, spoke with customers, and dug deeper into wellness science, we realized something fundamental: true balance doesn't start with what you wear. It starts with what fuels you from within.

That realization led us here. BalanceEQ represents our evolution—bringing the same obsessive attention to quality, the same commitment to authenticity, and the same pursuit of balance to cognitive wellness and performance nutrition.

The mission remains the same:

Help you find your balance, from the inside out.
